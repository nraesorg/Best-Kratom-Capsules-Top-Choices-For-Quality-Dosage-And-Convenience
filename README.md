## Frequently Asked Questions

### How long do kratom capsules take to work?

Kratom capsules typically take 30-45 minutes to show effects, as the capsule shell must dissolve in the stomach before the kratom powder can be absorbed. Taking capsules on an empty stomach may reduce onset time to 20-30 minutes, while consuming them with food may extend onset time to 45-60 minutes.

### What is the difference between gelatin and vegetarian kratom capsules?

Gelatin capsules are made from animal-derived collagen and dissolve slightly faster in stomach acid. Vegetarian capsules use plant-based materials like hydroxypropyl methylcellulose (HPMC) and are suitable for vegans and vegetarians. Both types effectively deliver kratom, with minimal difference in absorption rates.

### How many kratom capsules should a beginner take?

Beginners should start with 1-2 capsules containing 0.5-1 gram total kratom content. Wait at least 45-60 minutes to assess effects before taking additional capsules. New users should never exceed 2-3 grams in their first experience and should choose milder strains like Green Malay or Red Bali.

### Can kratom capsules lose potency over time?

Yes, kratom capsules can lose potency when exposed to heat, light, moisture, or air. Properly stored capsules in airtight containers away from direct sunlight and heat can maintain potency for 2-3 years. Signs of degradation include color changes, unusual odors, or diminished effects.

### Are kratom capsules more expensive than powder?

Kratom capsules typically cost more than equivalent amounts of powder due to additional processing, encapsulation equipment, and labor costs. However, capsules offer advantages in convenience, precise dosing, and taste masking that many users find worth the extra cost.

### How should kratom capsules be stored for maximum freshness?

Store kratom capsules in airtight containers in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Ideal storage temperature is 60-70°F with humidity below 60%. Avoid storing in bathrooms, cars, or areas with temperature fluctuations. Consider using desiccant packets for additional moisture protection.

### What makes some kratom capsules better quality than others?

High-quality kratom capsules feature lab-tested kratom powder, consistent capsule fill weights, proper encapsulation techniques, and comprehensive quality control. The best capsules include Certificates of Analysis, clear strain identification, proper packaging, and come from vendors with transparent business practices and good manufacturing standards.
